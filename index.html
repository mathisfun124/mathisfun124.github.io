AP Exam Free Reduction Initiative

SJUSD Is asking families regardless of income to complete the Free-Reduce Price Meal Application. When families complete the application, the cost of any Advance Placement (AP) exam will be reduced to $5, again regardless of income. to be eligible for this program, the application must be submitted by Wednesday, September 19, 2018. the application can be completed online at https://nutrition.sjusd.org.
